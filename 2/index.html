About Winder Walkers

Take control of a pair of wind-up toys and work together to solve puzzles and reach the goal, but be careful with your wind-up energy! Winder Walkers was created as an entry for Brackeys Jam 2020.2 by a team of 4. Of 1,803 entries, it came in 35th overall.
